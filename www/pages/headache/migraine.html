  • Triptans

    Zolmitriptan oral 5-10mg at onset, may repeat once if headache not resolved in two hours

    Note: VA/DoD mild TBI CPG recommends initiating dosing of Zolmitriptan oral at 5–10mg. While this may occur in practice, official drug manufacturer dosing recommendations suggest initiation with 2.5–5mg. Maximum dosing: 10mg/24 hours

  • Zolmitriptan nasal one spray of 5mg, may repeat once if headache not re- solved in two hours. Maximum daily dose should not exceed 10mg/day
  • Sumatriptan oral 50–100mg at onset, may repeat once if headache not resolved in two hours
  • Sumatriptan nasal one spray of 10mg, may repeat in two hours. Maximum daily dose should not exceed 40mg/day
  • Sumatriptan inject- able 6mg SQ, may repeat in one hour. Maximum daily dose not to exceed 12mg/day

  • Note: Abortive agents for migraine headaches should be given PRN at onset, no more than three days/week. Persistent usage can lead to rebound headaches.

  • Prophylactic agents

  • Divalproex sodium extended release 250mg BID. May increase by 250mg/day every week to maximum of 1000mg/day (draw levels)
  • Topiramate 25–100mg BID
  • Metoprolol 25mg BID. May increase every three to four weeks up to 100mg BID if needed
