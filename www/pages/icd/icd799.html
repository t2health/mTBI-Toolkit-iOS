  • Emotional/Behavioral Symptom Codes

  • 799-series codes allow providers to code emotional/behavioral symptoms without using mental health diagnosis codes
  • Do not replace mental health diagnosis codes
  • Used when providers observe symptoms but a mental health diagnosis is not established
